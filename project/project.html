HYDROPOWER

Energy conservation- the gift of mankind

The aim of our project is to produce electricity using water turbines. Energy conservation is the effort made to reduce the consumption of energy by using less of an energy service. Hydroelectricity is more economical and eco friendly as it produces very less greenhouse gases by which the level of pollution can be brought down. Hydropower plants are a vital energy source to the world. Water is an efficient and reliable fuel. The use, creation, and expansion of power plants should continue being pursued.
